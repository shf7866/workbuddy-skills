## 二、方法论文献

### 2.1 模型选择

- **BIC vs AIC**: Raftery, A.E. (1995). Bayesian Model Selection in Social Research. *Sociological Methodology*, 25, 111-163.
- **LMR-LRT**: Lo, Y., Mendell, N., & Rubin, D. (2001). Testing the Number of Components in a Normal Mixture. *Biometrika*, 88, 767-778.
- **BLRT**: McLachlan, G.J. (1987). On Bootstrapping the Likelihood Ratio Test Statistic. *JASA*, 82, 665-671.
- **模型选择模拟**: Nylund-Gibson, K., et al. (2014). Ten Frequently Asked Questions About Latent Class Analysis. *Assessment*, 21(5), 525-534.

### 2.2 测量等值性

- **LCA等值性**: Pastor, D.A., & Gagné, P. (2013). The Role of Measurement Invariance in Latent Class Analysis. In J.W. St Quant & C. Zumbo (Eds.), *Advancing Human Assessment*.
- **LTA等值性**: Collins, L.M., & Lanza, S.T. (2010). *Latent Class and Latent Transition Analysis*. Wiley.

### 2.3 外生变量整合

- **R3STEP方法**: Asparouhov, T., & Muthén, B. (2014). Auxiliary Variables in Mixture Modeling. *Structural Equation Modeling*, 21, 329-341.
- **BCH方法**: Bolck, A., Croon, M., & Hagenaars, J. (2004). Estimating Latent Structure Models. *Sociological Methods & Research*, 33, 291-318.
